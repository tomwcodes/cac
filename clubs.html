Lisvane Tennis Club
www.clubspark.lta.org.uk/LisvaneCACTennisClub

Lisvane Tennis Club is situated just north of Lisvane and Thornhill Railway Station. Tennis has been played at the current site since 1988.

We have 5 all-weather, floodlit courts (three artificial clay and two tarmac), in addition to a magnificent two storey club house. A large viewing balcony overlooks three courts and provides views across to neighbouring Parc Cefn Onn. We have a comprehensive coaching programme for all ages and levels.

Lisvane Tennis Club fields a number of teams in local leagues, and we have plenty of social open sessions (ladies, mens and mixed) both during the day and in the evenings. So if you are looking for a club in the local area that combines social and competitive play as well as coaching look no further than Lisvane Tennis Club. We look forward to seeing you.
