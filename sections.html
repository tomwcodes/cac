Our Sections

Cardiff Bowls Club
www.cardiffbowlsclub.com

The indoor season runs from mid September to mid April and the green is available daily from 10am. There is a large programme with separate daytime leagues for the ladies and gents with the latter also participating in evening leagues. In addition a mixed league takes place on Sunday evenings.

The club has been proud to have three of its members hold the office of President of the Welsh Indoor Bowls Association.


Cardiff Cricket Club
www.cardiff.play-cricket.com

Cardiff Cricket Club is the oldest cricket club in South Wales having celebrated our 200 year anniversary in 2019 and remains one of the leading cricket clubs in South Wales.

The Club runs four senior teams each Saturday and a midweek team which all play in the local leagues and there is a thriving junior section with teams at under 9, 11,13 and 15 with close links with local schools.

We are always looking for new players regardless of ability so please contact us if you are interested. During the season, senior outdoor training sessions will take place at the ground on Thursdays, 6-8pm.


Cardiff Hockey Club
www.cardiffandmet.co.uk

Cardiff & Met Hockey Club is based at the National Hockey Centre, Sophia Gardens near the city centre and at Cardiff Metropolitan University.

We have 5 Mens Senior Teams, 5 Ladies Senior Teams, 6 British Universities & Colleges Sports (BUCS) student teams and Juniors Teams from U9 to U18.


Cardiff Rugby Club
www.cardiffrfc.com

Cardiff RFC comes under the umbrella of the Cardiff Blues region (along with the Premiership sides of Merthyr and Pontypridd) and has so far produced several British Lions and Welsh internationals and a raft of players who have been capped at Regional level.

Cardiff RFC is committed to developing players to allow them to follow the path to play at the highest level possible.


Lisvane Tennis Club
www.clubspark.lta.org.uk/LisvaneCACTennisClub


Lisvane Tennis Club is situated just north of Lisvane and Thornhill Railway Station. Tennis has been played at the current site since 1988.

We have 5 all-weather, floodlit courts (three artificial clay and two tarmac), in addition to a magnificent two storey club house. A large viewing balcony overlooks three courts and provides views across to neighbouring Parc Cefn Onn. We have a comprehensive coaching programme for all ages and levels.

Lisvane Tennis Club fields a number of teams in local leagues, and we have plenty of social open sessions (ladies, mens and mixed) both during the day and in the evenings. So if you are looking for a club in the local area that combines social and competitive play as well as coaching look no further than Lisvane Tennis Club. We look forward to seeing you.
