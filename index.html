Welcome

Cardiff Athletic Club (CAC) is a multi-sport club comprising five sections (Bowls, Cricket, Hockey, Rugby Union and Tennis), and it is the premier amateur sports club in the capital city. CAC was established in 1922, although certain sections date back to the 19th century.

Whether you are a lifetime member or new to our Club, we welcome new ideas and value enthusiasm from members and volunteers. Please get in touch if you would like to enquire about becoming a member.
